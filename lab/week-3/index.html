Overview

A camera is an optical instrument used to capture images. Cameras may work with the light of the visible spectrum or with other portions of the electromagnetic spectrum.

There are various types of cameras, each with distinct features and purposes.

History

The history of the camera can be traced back much further than the introduction of photography. Cameras evolved from the camera obscura, an instrument that projected an image through a small hole onto a surface. The invention of photography in the early 19th century eventually led to the development of modern cameras.

Types

There are many types of cameras, including:

  • Digital cameras
  • Compact cameras
  • DSLR cameras
  • Mirrorless cameras
  • Medium format cameras
  • Action cameras
  • Instant cameras
